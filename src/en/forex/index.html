The foreign exchange market, commonly referred to as the forex market, stands as the largest and most liquid financial market globally. Forex trading involves speculating on the relative strength of one country's currency (known as the base currency) against another country's currency (known as the quote currency), with the aim of generating profits.

The foreign exchange (Forex) market holds the distinction of being the largest and most liquid market worldwide, with daily transaction volumes reaching staggering levels. It is estimated that the daily forex trading volume exceeds $5 trillion. This popularity stems from the market's high volatility, driven by the continuous movements of currency pairs.
